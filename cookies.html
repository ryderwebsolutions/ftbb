1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer, tablet or smartphone) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently, to remember your preferences and to provide information to the website owner about how their site is used.

Cookies cannot run programmes or deliver viruses to your device. They are uniquely assigned to your browser and can only be read by the web server that issued them.

3. How to Manage Cookies

You can manage or disable cookies in several ways:

  • Browser settings: All major browsers allow you to refuse, delete or be notified about cookies. Consult your browser's help documentation for instructions.

  • Google Analytics: Install the Google Analytics Opt-out Add-on in your browser.
  • On this site: Click the button below to reset your consent and see the cookie banner again.

Please note: disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality and performance of the website.
