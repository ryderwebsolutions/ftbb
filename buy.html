What's Inside the Guide

The FTBB guide is built specifically for the Irish property market — not adapted from a British or American template. Every chapter covers the exact schemes, rules, legal processes, and pitfalls that apply when buying in Ireland.

Whether you're just starting to think about buying or you're already in the process, the guide moves with you — giving you what you need at each stage.

01

Understanding Your Budget

Central Bank lending rules, income multiples, deposit requirements, and what you can actually borrow in 2025.

02

Mortgages — End to End

How mortgages work, how to compare lenders, the role of a broker, approval in principle, and what banks actually look at.

03

Help to Buy & First Home Scheme

Who qualifies, how to apply, how much you can claim, and the critical differences between the two schemes.

04

Saving Your Deposit

Real strategies for saving a 10% deposit in Ireland — including timelines, accounts, and the savings history banks want to see.

05

Legal Process & Conveyancing

What a solicitor does, what the contracts mean, what to watch for — and how to avoid the most common legal delays.

06

Surveys, Valuations & Insurance

Why you need a survey, which type to get, how mortgage protection works, and what home insurance must cover.

07

Common Mistakes & How to Avoid Them

Compiled from mortgage brokers and solicitors across Ireland — the errors that cost buyers time, money, and the home they wanted.

08

Moving In & What Happens After

Drawdown day, stamp duty, moving costs, SEAI grants for your new home, and what to do in the first six months.

Why Buyers Use the FTBB Guide

🎯

Clear, Not Complicated

The Irish buying process is confusing by default. The FTBB guide cuts through the jargon and explains every step in plain English — so you know exactly what's happening and what to do next.

🇮🇪

Built for the Irish Market

Central Bank rules, Help to Buy, First Home Scheme, Revenue mortgage credit, solicitor costs, stamp duty — everything is specific to buying property in the Republic of Ireland, not imported from the UK or elsewhere.

💶

Avoid Costly Mistakes

First-time buyers lose time and money on preventable errors — poor solicitor choice, missed grants, weak mortgage applications. The guide covers the most common mistakes in detail so you don't repeat them.

📋

Used Alongside Your Advisors

The guide doesn't replace your mortgage broker or solicitor. It prepares you for every conversation — so you ask the right questions, understand the answers, and don't have to rely on others to explain everything from scratch.

🔄

Up to Date for 2025

Irish mortgage rules, grant scheme limits, and government support programmes change regularly. The FTBB guide reflects the current rules as of 2025, including the latest Central Bank lending limits and Help to Buy changes.

✅

Checklists for Every Stage

Each chapter includes practical checklists so you know exactly what to do, what to have ready, and what to ask — from your first mortgage inquiry to the day you collect your keys.

Who It's For

Written for Anyone Who Is Serious About Buying Their First Home in Ireland
