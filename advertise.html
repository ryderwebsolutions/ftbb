The FTBB Audience

A High-Intent Audience in Active Purchase Mode

First-time buyers are not passive readers. They come to FTBB with specific questions at each stage of their journey — and they act on the answers. At the point of reading, they are choosing service providers, comparing products, and making decisions that will shape the next decade of their financial lives.

The businesses that reach them here, in a context of trust and expert guidance, establish relationships that last well beyond the initial purchase.

  • Comparing mortgage lenders and speaking to brokers
  • Selecting a solicitor for their conveyancing
  • Evaluating surveyors, valuers, and engineers
  • Comparing home insurance and mortgage protection
  • Planning kitchens, furniture, flooring, and home upgrades
  • Researching SEAI energy grants for their new property
