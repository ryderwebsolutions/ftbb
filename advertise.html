Who Reads FTBB

A Highly Targeted Audience in Active Purchase Mode

First-time buyers in Ireland are one of the most valuable consumer audiences in the country. At the point of purchase, they are not just buying a home — they are selecting mortgage providers, solicitors, surveyors, insurers, and a full range of home products and services, often for the first time.

FTBB reaches these buyers through expert guides, practical tools, and a trusted newsletter — at exactly the moment they are making these decisions.

  • Researching mortgage lenders and comparing rates
  • Selecting a solicitor for their conveyancing
  • Evaluating surveyors, valuers, and structural engineers
  • Comparing mortgage protection and home insurance options
  • Planning furniture, kitchens, flooring, and home improvements
  • Considering solar, energy upgrades, and SEAI grants
  • Researching moving companies and related services

Why First-Time Buyers Are a Valuable Audience

01

High Spending Intent, All at Once

First-time buyers are purchasing a mortgage, legal services, insurance, surveys, furniture, and appliances — often simultaneously. The volume of purchasing decisions concentrated into a 6–12 month window is unlike almost any other consumer moment.

02

They Are Actively Seeking Expert Guidance

Unlike repeat buyers who know the process, first-time buyers are explicitly looking for trusted recommendations. They consult FTBB guides before selecting service providers — which means your presence here carries genuine authority.

03

Long-Term Relationship Potential

The customer you reach at the point of their first purchase becomes a long-term customer. Mortgage holders switch, remortgage, and refer. Home insurance renews. Solicitors get referred to family and friends. The lifetime value of a first-time buyer customer is substantial.
