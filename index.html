What Is FTBB

Ireland's Dedicated Resource Hub For First-Time Property Buyers

FTBB — First Time Buyer's Bible — is Ireland's most comprehensive resource platform for people navigating their first property purchase. We don't sell mortgages. We don't push products. We connect buyers with the right expertise, at the right time.

From understanding your borrowing capacity under Central Bank lending rules, to navigating the Help to Buy scheme, finding a trusted solicitor, and completing your mortgage drawdown — FTBB guides you through every stage of the Irish property journey.

  • Mortgage guides, rate comparisons & broker introductions
  • Government grants: Help To Buy & First Home Scheme
  • Solicitor, surveyor & insurance partner network
  • Step-by-step buyer roadmap from budget to keys
  • Up-to-date market insights for Irish first-time buyers
