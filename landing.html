Why FTBB

Built for the Irish market

Not generic advice from the UK or US — this guide is written specifically for the Irish property market, Irish lenders, and Irish law.

Ireland-specific from cover to cover

Written around Irish Central Bank lending rules, Revenue HTB relief, the NPPR and PRTB registers, and how Irish solicitors handle conveyancing. No irrelevant UK content.

Up to date for 2025

HTB limits, First Home Scheme equity caps, current AIP criteria and stamp duty rates — all current as of the 2025 Budget and Finance Act.

Written by buyers, for buyers

FTBB was created by a team of Irish first-time buyers who went through the process. Every section is written around the questions people actually ask — not the ones advisers wish they'd ask.

No paid recommendations

Our editorial content is 100% independent. Partners who advertise on FTBB are clearly labelled. Our guides never recommend specific lenders, solicitors or brokers without disclosure.
